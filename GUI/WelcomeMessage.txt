Welcome to Rak!

Rak is a free,fast & realistic renderer designed to work on any gpu. 
Rak is licensed under the CC0 license, so feel free to use the software for anything! 

If this is your first time using the software, feel free to check out the documentation!

If you wish to check out the github, you can do so by click the button at the bottom of this tab!

If you need some help with navigating the software, feel free to click the help button on the top-left corner!
